Hot Finger
Sample Hot Finger Enabled Content

Drag your finger or mouse over this. The content is wrapped in a Hot Finger Widget, which turns any HTML content into a heat map.

The heat map intends to illustrate where the viewers are interacting with the content. The size of an orange hotspot correlates to how many viewers are touching that area.

Create your own Hot Finger Widget with the instructions below. Wrap your content in the customized code snippet you recieve, and you will have a customized heat map that will show in real time where your viewers are touching your content.

The magic is in the web sockets.
